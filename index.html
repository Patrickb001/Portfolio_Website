Mapty Application

The mapty application targets users who want to keep track of their workouts in their nearby area. You can open the mapty application, and after allowing the app to use your location you can see the city you are in and get started. If you want to log your running or cycling workouts you can by clicking a spot on the map where you workout and input the information into the form. Once you submit the form it is saved on the application and you can keep track over time of your workouts this way!
